Market Insight

The numbers
are undeniable.

  • 78% of Canadians report anxiety before dental appointments. The waiting room is where that anxiety peaks. A brand that reframes this moment with play creates an emotional association money can't buy through traditional advertising.
  • Dave & Buster's Canadian locations are distributed in major metros. Dental offices are everywhere else — in every suburb, every mid-sized city D&B doesn't yet reach. This is a geographic bridge to untapped markets.
  • Kids are the multiplier. A child who plays a D&B game in the waiting room creates an average of 2.3 family conversations about visiting a location. Pediatric and family dental practices are the highest-priority targets for Phase 1.
  • Dental patients skew middle-to-upper income — the same demographic that spends on entertainment. The income profile of a dental patient is D&B's core customer. These audiences overlap almost perfectly.
D&B Dental Arcade Unit
The Product

What goes
in the room.

The D&B Dental Arcade Unit is purpose-built for the waiting room — compact, family-friendly, and unmistakably Dave & Buster's. Piloted first at Eglinton West Dental.

Display 15" HD touch screen — sanitizable surface, glare-resistant, ADA compliant. Wall-mount or countertop. 24" × 16" footprint.
Game Library 12–15 family-friendly titles updated quarterly over WiFi. No violent content. Age-appropriate for all waiting room demographics.
Chip Rewards Physical D&B chip dispenser built-in. Patients earn 5–20 chips per session, redeemable at any Canadian D&B location.
SMS Integration Patients text their chip balance to carry it to any D&B location. No app required.
Analytics D&B ops team sees plays, chip redemptions, and engagement data by location in real time.
Compliance PIPEDA-compliant. Zero patient data collected. No login required. Designed for healthcare waiting rooms.
